About Me

I am a Fullstack Software Developer based in Berlin who is passionate about fixing our day to day problems by the applied use of Technology. Since more and more businesses are digitising their medium of communication with their customers, I understand the demand for the skills required and ready to take up the challenge.

My core skill sets are that I am a Quick Learner, Team-player and I am open to challenges. And my technical skills are HTML, CSS and JavaScript and their related frameworks.

My journey as a software developer started with self-learning on Freecodecamp. In order to bring more structure to my learning, I joined Digital Career Institute for the Full-stack Developer course. Freecodecamp and Digital Career Institute have accelerated this journey and I've been able to work on various software projects both independently and as part of the program.

I am looking for something interesting and challenging where I can use my skills to solve meaningful problems, learn from colleagues.

My Core Skills

I am specialised in developing responsive websites for businesses and for individuals. Websites don't have to be static, I love making pages come to life. My highest priority is for Fast load times and lag free interaction.

I have a product-oriented development mindset and a responsible ownership attitude towards software components.
